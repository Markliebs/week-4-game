Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random target number at the start of each round.

There are 4 crystals below.

You win the round by matching your total score to the target, you lose if your total score goes over it.

Click on a crystal to add it's value to your total. The value of each crystal will not be shown to you.

When a new round starts, a new target number is chosen and new random values will be assigned to each crystal.

Good Luck, And May The Force Be With You, Always.
