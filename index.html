Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are 4 crystals below.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose if your total score goes over the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, a new random number is chosen.

Good Luck!
